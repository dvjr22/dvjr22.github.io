A little about me...

I'm an inquisitive, energetic computer scientist with a passion for data collection/management and software design.

I'm a senior at the University of South Carolina in Columbia majoring in computer science. My experience includes designing and developing Android applications in a team environment under strict deadlines, databases to be implemented in academic and real world situations, and incorporating multiple computer aspects to create a unified system to solve and prevent problems.

What I most enjoy about the technology field is having the opportunity to be part of providing solutions to current problems. The work I've done has allowed me to continue to learn, grow as both a person and a computer scientist, and possibly contribute multiple diciplines.

In my spare time, I enjoy reading(fiction, nonfiction, graphic novels), playing video games, and doing other stuff.
